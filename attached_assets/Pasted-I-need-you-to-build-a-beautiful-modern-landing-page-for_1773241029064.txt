- In the Hero, arrange images in a scattered/collage style — slightly rotated (~2–4deg), overlapping, with mixed sizes — creating a dynamic editorial feel
- Add subtle drop shadows on floating image cards (shadow-lg)
- Avoid full-width image banners; prefer contained image blocks with negative space around them

### Layout & Spacing
- Max content width: 1200px, centered, with px-8 gutters
- Section vertical padding: py-24 minimum
- Cards: p-8, rounded-2xl, border border-gray-100, hover:shadow-md transition

### Interaction & Motion

- Cards: hover:shadow-lg, hover:border-[#32BF50] subtle transition
- Prompt suggestion items (Section 3): hover:bg-green-50, hover:text-[#32BF50], with left-border accent animation
- Section entrance: subtle fade-in-up animation on scroll (use Intersection Observer or Framer Motion)

### Navigation

### Green CTA Section (Section 5) specifics

### Footer
